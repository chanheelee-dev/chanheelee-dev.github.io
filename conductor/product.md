# Initial Concept
Personal blog document repository for chanheelee-dev.github.io, focusing on writings describing the author.

# Product Guide
## Vision
A personal blog and digital garden to document and showcase professional and personal growth as a developer and thinker.

## Objectives
- Showcase technical projects and professional portfolio.
- Share practical, referenceable technical articles.
- Express a unique identity as both a "Builder" (technician) and a "Thinker" (philosopher/observer).
- Bridge the gap between detailed technical records on GitHub and broader-audience insights on Medium.

## Core Features
- Static site generated with Jekyll.
- Organized content: Essays, Projects, and a landing page.
- SEO optimization and RSS feed support.
- Mathematical notation support with MathJax.
- Integration with Netlify for deployment.

## Audience
- Developers looking for technical insights and practical guides.
- Potential employers and collaborators interested in the author's work.
- The author's personal digital presence for long-term record-keeping.
