# Product Guidelines

## Content Strategy
- Focus on clarity, authenticity, and technical depth.
- Use a professional yet personal tone, bridging the gap between a builder and a thinker.
- Prioritize high-quality, long-form content over frequent, low-impact updates.

## Design and UX
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic inspired by the Jekyll Primer theme.
- Responsive design for optimal viewing across all devices.
- Seamless navigation between essays, projects, and the main landing page.
- Focus on accessibility and readability (typography, contrast, etc.).

## SEO and Discoverability
- Use descriptive, keywords-rich titles and metadata.
- Ensure all posts have proper front matter for SEO and social sharing tags.
- Regularly update the sitemap and RSS feed.

## Development Workflow
- Follow a consistent content structure for both essays and projects.
- Use version control for all content and configuration changes.
- Rigorously test locally using `jekyll serve` before deploying to production.
